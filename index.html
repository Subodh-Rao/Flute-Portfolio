About Me

I am Subodh P. Rao, hailing from Sagar, Karnataka, and I have honed my flute skills for over 13 years, mastering both Carnatic and Hindustani classical forms. As a freelance flute artist, I have performed a diverse range of concerts, spanning from classical compositions to contemporary film music, captivating audiences with my versatility and passion for the art.

Clasical Concerts

A performance showcasing the rich heritage of Hindustani and Carnatic classical music, highlighting traditional ragas and compositions. These recitals explore the depth and intricacies of classical flute playing, evoking the soulful essence of Indian music through intricate improvisations and deeply meditative tones.

Private Concerts

Experience the magic of film music through the soulful sounds of the flute. In these exclusive private concerts, I blend the timeless melodies of iconic film songs with the rich tonal beauty of classical flute playing. From evocative romantic ballads to lively upbeat tunes, the versatility of the flute brings a unique texture to your favorite cinematic scores. Whether it’s an intimate gathering or a special celebration, these performances promise to evoke emotions and create unforgettable moments through the enchanting voice of the flute.

Jugalbandi Concerts

A celebration of the unique relationship between the human voice and the flute, this Jugalbandi explores the concept of ‘Gayaki Angh’—the flute imitating vocal nuances. In collaboration with a vocalist, my flute mirrors the tonal and emotive qualities of the voice, enhancing each raaga's mood and depth
